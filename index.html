Paradise Island Sea Trip with Red Sea Sun sea Hurghada 🌴🚤

What is Orange Bay? Who wants to visit the Maldives of Egypt? This tour is specially designed for you! This trip is crafted to provide you with pure joy and complete relaxation through a perfect blend of water and breathtaking nature. The golden sands of Orange Bay are decorated with wooden palm-frond umbrellas, sun loungers, bean bags, wooden walkways, and beach huts. Program Orange Bay Island from Hurghada – You will be picked up from your hotel and transferred to New Marina Port, where you will set sail towards the most beautiful coral reefs of Hurghada. On your way to Orange Bay Island, you will stop several times for snorkeling to enjoy the vibrant marine life, colorful fish, and corals, as well as for exciting water sports such as banana boats and sofa boats. After that, you will spend around three hours on the stunning Orange Bay Island, where you can explore, relax, and enjoy a delicious lunch served on board the boat. Finally, as your day comes to an end, the boat will take you back to New Marina Port, from where you will be transferred to your hotel.

🐢 Sea Turtles in Abu Dabbab Abu Dabbab Beach is one of the best places in the world to see giant sea turtles swimming gracefully in the crystal-clear waters of the Red Sea. These fascinating creatures move peacefully and offer you a truly unique experience while swimming or snorkeling alongside them. You may also encounter the rare and gentle Dugong (Sea Cow), a favorite among visitors. That’s why a trip to Abu Dabbab is not just a beach day, but an unforgettable natural adventure that combines relaxation with exploration.
